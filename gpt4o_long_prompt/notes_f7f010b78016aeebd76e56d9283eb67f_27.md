77. **gold, golden**  
   gold 指金制品, golden 指金色的, 但金鱼用 gold fish。a gold ring.

78. **most, mostly**  
   most 用在最高级的副词前, 相当于 very, 多半的, 大多数的用 mostly。  
   A most reliable man; mostly 老师, work in locality. most people; the people are mostly.

79. **just, very**  
   just 表达到的真实词, 作形容, "刚" 表示强调用 just 而摩题用 very。  
   the very man, just the man.

80. **wide, broad**  
   wide 用在事、立体形象上, 如 distance; broadigular[面]形而用 broad 如 shoulders。

81. **real, true**  
   real 真假, 真的, 指的是事实或非本质是假象而非应用。假的。真相。真目的。

82. **respectful, respectable**  
   respectful 恭敬的; 有礼貌的。respectable 可做的, 值得尊敬的。  
   be respectful to be the aged.

83. **outwards, outward**  
   向外做, 向外面, 方向, 外面向。 outward 表示向[面/準原形的情况]。

84. **pleasant, pleased, pleasing**  
   pleasant 作形容词, pleased, pleasing 作用不需要。pleased 作事物方法。  
   things is pleasant to. a pleasant trip. The trip is pleasing.

85. **understanding, understandable**  
   understanding 则指理解的精, 可作使; understandable 可理解卜理解匀。  
   An understanding girl; an understandable mistake.
