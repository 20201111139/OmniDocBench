10. 引导结果状语从句的从属连词有: so that (结果) 和 so/such...that (功能: 结果状语)

It was very cold, so that the water in the bowl froze.

It is such a heavy box that nobody can move it.

11. 引导比较状语从句的从属连词有：as ... as (同, 一样), not as/so...as (不同, 取不比) 和 than (比)...

I know you better than she does.

He writes as carefully as she.

I can't run as/so fast as you.

12. 引导名词性从句的从属连词有：that 和 if/whether (是否) 等。

We know that the earth goes around the sun. (陈述从句)

Whether he'll go there hasn't been decided. (名词性从句)

[注重了名词性从句和句各作状语从句中的, 由某句日属状语联属, 从句里用 - 复现各时。
