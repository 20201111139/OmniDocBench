```markdown
**look** 看/从表面上看，seem 指从内心的判断，appear 指给人从表面印象看。

appear wise, look like his father.

107. **gather / collect**

gather 指有目的和需要去收集/collect 指选择他物 有没有条件要收集

collect stamps.

108. **mean to do, mean doing**

mean to do 计划，准备做某事；mean doing 意思是，代表着

By this I mean giving the students more practice.

109. **die from, die of**

die from 表示死于（外伤、疾病），指患者意外受伤致使最终 / die of 表示死于（内因），指死于饥饿、疾病、衰老等

die of hunger and cold.

110. **pay for, pay back, pay off**

pay for 付费, pay back 还清, 但不包括清债 pay off 清债

pay for the book, pay off the debt.

111. **divide, separate**

divide 把一整体分成多部分 separate 让整体变成与之相邻的分开

divide the apple, separate the houses.

112. **arrive, get, reach**

arrive 不及物动词，听不加介词（大城市上是）; get (小地方) 不加介词；reach 抵达及物动词

arrive in Beijing, get to Beijing, reach Beijing.

113. **grow, plant**

grow 强调植物自然而稳定增长不需要人参与；plant 强调施行/栽培技巧

放植物而感知成长. Plant the trees, these are growing.

114. **manage, try**

manage to do 强调成功去做某事；try to do 只努力去做某事，但不一定成功。

He tried to pass the exam, but he failed.
```
