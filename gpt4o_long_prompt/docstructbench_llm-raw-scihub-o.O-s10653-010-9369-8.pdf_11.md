Spatial and temporal source identification in surface water

Cluster analysis identified two spatial groups of sites. Group 1 comprised L2, L4, L7, L9, and L10, and group 2 comprised L1, L3, L5, L6, and L8 (Figs. 3 and 4). These groups exhibited distinct heterogeneity in metal and nutrient concentrations. Group 1 showed relatively greater concentration of metals, whereas group 2 showed high contents of nutrients such as \( \text{Cl}^{-} \), \( \text{SO}_4^{2-} \), \( \text{NO}_3{-}\text{N} \), and \( \text{NH}_4{-}\text{N} \) (Fig. 4).
