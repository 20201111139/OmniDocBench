## 海伦公式

公式：\( S_{\Delta} = \sqrt{p(p-a)(p-b)(p-c)} \)

\( p = \frac{a+b+c}{2} \)，\( a, b, c \) 为三角形边长

那么：

\[
S_{\Delta ABC} = \sqrt{\frac{11}{2} \left(\frac{11}{2} - 2\right) \left(\frac{11}{2} - 3\right) \left(\frac{11}{2} - 5\right)}
\]
\( p = \frac{2+4+5}{2} = \frac{11}{2} \)

\[ 
= \sqrt{\frac{11}{2} \times \frac{7}{2} \times \frac{3}{2} \times \frac{1}{2}} \\
= \frac{\sqrt{231}}{4} 
\]

**因此：**

\[
S_{PQE} = \frac{1}{2} \times CQ \times DP
\]
\[
= \frac{1}{2} \times 2 \times DP = DP
\]

**所以：** 

\[ DP = \frac{\sqrt{231}}{4} \] 符合 \(\text{根捡 ①}\)

\[ 
S_{三角形 ABC} = 9 \times 高 = 9 DP = \frac{9 \sqrt{231}}{4} 
\]

**根捡（\(\triangle\)）**
\[
\frac{S_{MAD}}{S_{MAD} + \frac{9\sqrt{231}}{4}} = \frac{16}{25} 
\]

**因此：**

\[ S_{\Delta MAD} = 4 \sqrt{231} \quad (\text{end!}) \]
