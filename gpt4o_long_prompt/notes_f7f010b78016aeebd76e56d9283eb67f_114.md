### 中考作文常见结尾模板

#### 一、解决方法题型

整套事件与问题结束同样的结语给予

1. 问题现状

2. 怎样解决（解决方案的优缺点）

In recent days, we have to face a problem — ___A___, which is becoming more and more serious. First, ______ (说明A的现状).

Second, ______ (举例说明现状).

Confronted with A, we should take a series of effective measures to cope with the situation. For one thing, ____ (解决方法一). For another, ____ (解决方法二). Finally, ____ (解决方法三).

Personally, I believe that _____ (我的观点). Consequently, I'm confident that a bright future is awaiting us because ______ (带来的好处).

1. 说明事物现状

2. 事物本身的优缺点（或一方面）

3. 你对事物前景的看法
