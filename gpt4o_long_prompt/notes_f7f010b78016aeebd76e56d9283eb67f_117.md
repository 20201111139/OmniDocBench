1. "A or B" 选择性作文模板：

写作.第一段: Some people hold the opinion that A is superior to B in many ways. Others, however, argue that B is much better. Personally, I would prefer A because I think A has more advantages.

支持: 第二段: There are many reasons why I prefer A. The main reason is that ____. Another reason is that ____ (理由A的原因).

驳斥: 第三段: Of course, B also has advantage to some extent… (驳回句1: 1个B的劣势).

结论: 总结段: But if all these factors are considered, A is much better than B. From what has been discussed above, we may finally draw the conclusion that ____ (结尾总结句).

2. 观点或立场作文模板：

写作.第一段: 强烈的赞同或支持某个原作作为立场的作文题目。 As a student, I am strongly in favour of the decision (表明自己的立场是强烈反对或支持).

原因: 第二段: The reasons for this may be listed as follows: (其原因，应有实际例子).

正反: 论述: First of all… Secondly… Besides… (列出2-3个原因并加以说明).

结论: 总结段: In conclusion, I believe that… (应该需要、总结态度，给出结论).

4. "How to" 构成化文模板：

写作.第一段: 提到一个或几个解决问题的方法为作文的题目。 

正反: Many ways can help to solve these serious problems, but the following may be most effective. First of all, ____ Another way to solve the problem is ____. Finally, ____ (列出2-3个解决问题的方法).
