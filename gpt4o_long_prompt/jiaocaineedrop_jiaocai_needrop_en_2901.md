### 7 Complete the sentences with the correct form of the verbs in brackets.

1. If you _______ (not understand), _______ (ask) your teacher.

2. She _______ (not be) satisfied unless her son _______ (win) first place.

3. If you _______ (see) a red light, _______ (not cross) the road.

4. You _______ (not feel) relaxed unless you _______ (learn) to let go.

* 1 don't understand, ask  
* 2 won't be, wins  
* 3 see, don't cross  
* 4 won't feel, learn
