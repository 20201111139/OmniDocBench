# Enclosed Basic Switches

North American limit switches are also available in an enclosed basic version: These switches are designated by the catalog number 3SEO3-EB. Enclosed basic switches are preconfigured with a plunger actuator; booted plunger; roller lever; booted roller lever; roller plunger; or a booted roller plunger:
