majorchemicals frequentlypresentinWwTP effluents, which also show high toxicities. Kadokami et al. Consequently, the biological method (i.e., WET testing), which measures the potential andreal toxicity ofmixed chemicals,should be used for monitoring the toxicity of wwTP efluents containing varioushazardouschemicals.
