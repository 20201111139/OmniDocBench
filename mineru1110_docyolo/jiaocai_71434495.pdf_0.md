$$
{\underset{\mathsf{N}}{\mathop{\searrow}}}={\frac{1}{2}}\int_{0}^{\frac{1}{2}}{\frac{1}{2t^{2}-2t-4}}~\mathrm{d}t={\frac{1}{12}}\int_{0}^{\frac{1}{2}}\left({\frac{1}{t-2}}-{\frac{1}{t+1}}\right)\mathrm{d}t=-{\frac{\ln2}{12}}
$$

$$

$$

$$
\ln(1+x)=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{\frac{(-1)^{n-1}}{n}}x^{n},x\in(-1,1].
$$

$$
f(x)=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{\frac{(-1)^{n}}{2n+1}}x^{2n+1},x\in[-1,1].
$$

$$
f^{\prime}(x)=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}(-1)^{n}x^{2n}=-{\frac{x^{2}}{1+x^{2}}}={\frac{1}{1+x^{2}}}-1,x\in(-1,1).
$$  

即  $f(x)=\arctan x-x.$  故  

$$
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{\frac{(-1)^{n-1}}{n(2n+1)}}=\ln2+2f(1)=\ln2+2\left({\frac{\pi}{4}}-1\right)=\ln2+{\frac{\pi}{2}}-2
$$
