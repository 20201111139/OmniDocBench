### 53

记住这个公式你就可以口算！

**海伦公式：** \( S_{\triangle} = \sqrt{p(p-a)(p-b)(p-c)} \)

\( (p = \frac{a+b+c}{2}, a, b, c为三角形三边) \)

那么：\( S_{\triangle ABC} = \sqrt{\frac{11}{2} \cdot \frac{11-2}{2} \cdot \frac{11-4}{2} \cdot \frac{11-5}{2}} \)

\( (p = \frac{2+4+5}{2} = \frac{11}{2}) \)

\( = \sqrt{\frac{11}{2} \cdot \frac{7}{2} \cdot \frac{3}{2} \cdot \frac{1}{2}} \)

\( = \frac{\sqrt{231}}{4} \)

又：\( S_{\triangle ABC} = \frac{1}{2} \times c \times DP \)

\( = \frac{1}{2} \times 2 \times DP = DP \)

\( \therefore DP = \frac{\sqrt{231}}{4} \) 根据①

\( S_{\triangle ABD} = 9 \times 高 = 9 \times DP = \frac{9\sqrt{231}}{4} \)

根据② \( \frac{S_{\triangle ABD}}{S_{\triangle ABD} + \frac{9\sqrt{231}}{4}} = \frac{16}{25} \)

\( \Rightarrow S_{\triangle ABD} = 4\sqrt{231} \) (end！)
