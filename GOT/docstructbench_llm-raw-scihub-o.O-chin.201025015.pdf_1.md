25-015 Mixed Cations and Structural Complexity in \(\left(\mathrm{Eu}_{1-x} \mathrm{Ca}_{y}\right)_{4} \mathrm{In}_{3} \mathrm{Ge}_{2}\) and
\(\left(\mathrm{Eu}_{1-x} \mathrm{Ca}_{y}\right) \mathrm{In}_{2} \mathrm{Ge}_{3}-\) The First Two Members of the Homologous Series
\(\mathrm{A}_{310 \mathrm{~nm}} \mathrm{In}_{2 \mathrm{~nm}} \mathrm{Ge}_{2 \mathrm{~nm}} \mathrm{In}_{1} \mathrm{~m}, \mathbf{m}=1,2, \cdots \infty ; \mathrm{A}: \mathrm{Ca}, \mathrm{Sr}, \mathrm{Ba}, \mathrm{Eu}\), or \(\mathrm{Yb}\) ).
\(\mathrm{Eu}_{1.2 x} \mathrm{Ca}_{2.77} \mathrm{In}_{3} \mathrm{Ge}_{4}(\mathrm{I}), \mathrm{Eu}_{2.60} \mathrm{Ca}_{1.40} \mathrm{In}_{3} \mathrm{Ge}_{4}\) (II), \(\mathrm{Eu}_{0.30} \mathrm{Ca}_{2.70} \mathrm{In}_{2} \mathrm{Ge}_{3}\) (III), and
\(\mathrm{Eu}_{0.6} \mathrm{Ca}_{2.34} \mathrm{In}_{2} \mathrm{Ge}_{3}\) (IV) are synthesized from the elements in a molten In metal flux
\(\left(960^{\circ} \mathrm{C}, 20 \mathrm{~h}\right)\). The compounds are characterized by single crystal XRD, magnetic susceptibility measurements, and TB-LMTO band structure calculations. (I) and (II) crystallize in the space group \(\mathrm{C} 2 / \mathrm{m}\) with \(Z=2\), and (III) and (IV) in the space group Pnma with \(Z=4\). The polyanionic substructures of the compounds are based on \(\mathrm{InGe}_{4}\) edgedsharing tetrahedra, \(\mathrm{Ge}_{2}\) dimers, and bridging \(\mathrm{In}\) atoms in a nearly square planar environment. - (YOU, T.-S.; TOBASH, P.
