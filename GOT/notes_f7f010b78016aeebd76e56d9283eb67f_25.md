56. the people, a people
the people 指人, a people 指民族. The Chinese is a peace-loving people.
57. it, one
注同一物律, one 同类不同一. I lost my pen. I have to buy a new one.
58. that, thus
that指作上文所提到的, 比么事断下文所要选的.
I was it, That's why
59. none, nothing, no one
none 强调有等少, nothing, no one 强调有没有, nothing指物, no相指人.
- How many ... / How much ... ? - none
bo. anyone, any one
anowere指人,不能接的; any one指人、物均可, 可接的. any one of yau.
bl. who, what
Who指姓名或关系, what指联业或地位
What is your dad? He is a teacher.
62. what, which
What 的选择基础是限制的。which 在一定范围内进行选择.
which do you prefer, bananas or apples?
63. other, another
other后接名词受数, another后接名词学数. other students, another student.
64. not a little, not a bit.
not a like非常, not a bit一点也不.
I'm not a bit tired.我一点儿也不累.
65. many, much, a lot of
many和可数名词连用, much和不可数名词连用, a lot of 可数.
但不用于否定句. 7 haven't many books.
