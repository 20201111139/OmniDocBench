\section*{Authority for Conducting the Matching Program}
The authority for conducting the matching program is 42 U.S.C. 653 (j)(3).
Purpose(s)
The purpose of the matching program is to provide each participating state agency administering TANF with new hire, quarterly wage, and unemployment insurance information from OCSE's NDNH system of records to assist them in establishing or verifying the number of people's employment liability for assistance, reducing payment errors, and maintaining program integrity, including the number of people's name of the participation exists or if the applicant or recipient resides in another state. The state TANF agencies may also use the NDNH information for the secondary purpose of updating the recipients' reported participation in work activities and updating recipients' and their employers' contact information for the agency.
Categories of Individual TANF agencies.
The categories of individuals involved in the matching program are adult members of households who have applied for or receive TANF benefits.
