奇点分类 (III)
}

- \(\Delta=0 \mathrm{~A}\), 有两个相同的特征值

\[
\begin{array}{l}
\mathbf{P}^{\otimes}_{\xi \xi}\left(\begin{array}{cc}
1 & 2
\end{array}\right) \quad \mathbf{P}^{-1} \mathbf{A P}=\left(\begin{array}{c}
\lambda \\
\lambda
\end{array}\right) \\
\left\{\begin{array}{l}
\frac{d u}{d t}=\lambda u \\
\frac{d v}{d t}=\lambda v
\end{array}\right\} \begin{array}{l}
u=u_{0} e^{\lambda t} \\
v=v_{0} e^{\lambda t}
\end{array} \begin{array}{l}
\frac{u}{v}=\frac{u_{0}}{v_{0}}
\end{array}
\end{array}
\]
\[
\begin{array}{l}
\alpha<0, \lambda<0 \\
\alpha>0, \lambda>0
\end{array}
\]
