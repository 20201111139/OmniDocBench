奇点分类（III）

- $\Delta = 0$ 时，有两个相同的特征值

$$P = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 1 & 2 \end{pmatrix}, \quad P^{-1}AP = \begin{pmatrix} \lambda & \lambda \\ \lambda & \lambda \end{pmatrix}^2$$

$$\frac{du}{dt} = \lambda u \quad \frac{dv}{dt} = \lambda v$$

$$u = u_0 e^{\lambda t}, \quad v = v_0 e^{\lambda t}, \quad u = \frac{u_0}{v_0}$$

- $\alpha < 0, \lambda < 0$
- $\alpha > 0, \lambda > 0$

$$\frac{du}{dt} = P^{-1}APu$$

$$\alpha = \text{tr}(A) = \lambda_1 + \lambda_2$$

$$\beta = |A| = \lambda_1 \lambda_2$$
