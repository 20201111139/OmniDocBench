则 \( P(X = 0) = \frac{C_0^2 C_3^3}{C_0^3} = \frac{4}{84} = \frac{1}{21}, \quad P(X = 1) = \frac{C_1^1 C_3^2}{C_0^3} = \frac{30}{84} = \frac{5}{14}, \)

\[P(X = 2) = \frac{C_2^2 C_4^1}{C_0^3} = \frac{40}{84} = \frac{10}{21}, \quad P(X = 3) = \frac{C_3^3 C_4^0}{C_0^3} = \frac{10}{84} = \frac{5}{42},\]

| X | 0  | 1  | 2  | 3  |
|---|----|----|----|----|
| P | \frac{1}{21} | \frac{5}{14} | \frac{10}{21} | \frac{5}{42} |
