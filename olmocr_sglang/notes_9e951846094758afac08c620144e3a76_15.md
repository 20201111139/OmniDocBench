（P为：\( P = \frac{a+b+c}{2} \)，a, b, c 为三角形三边长）

那么：

\[ S_{\Delta DQC} = \sqrt{\frac{11}{2} \cdot (\frac{11}{2} - 2) \cdot (\frac{11}{2} - 4) \cdot (\frac{11}{2} - 5)} \]
\( P = \frac{2 + 4 + 5}{2} = \frac{11}{2} \)

\[ = \sqrt{\frac{11}{2} \cdot \frac{7}{2} \cdot \frac{3}{2} \cdot \frac{1}{2}} \]
\[ = \frac{\sqrt{231}}{4} \]

\[ S_{\Delta DQC} = \frac{1}{2} \times (CQ \times DP) \]

\[ DP = \frac{\sqrt{231}}{4} \]
