gold, golden
gold 指真金制品, golden 指金色的, 但金鱼用 gold fish, a gold ring.

most, mostly
most 用于表感受的肯定句中, 相当于 very, 与大部分大多数解时显形多词或名词; mostly 大部分, 显副词; most people, the people are mostly

just, very
just 表强调时显副词, 作状语; very 表强调时显形容词, 用作定语; the very man, just the man.

wide, broad
wide 侧重于一边到另一边的距离, broad 侧重于画面的宽广, broad shoulders.

real, true
real 真的, 真实的, 指的是事实上存在而不是想象的; true 真的, 正确的, 指的是事实和实际情况相符合, real gold, a true story.

respectful, respectable
respectful 尊敬的, 有礼貌的; respectable 可敬的, 值得尊敬的, be respectful to be the aged.

outwards, outward
二者均可用作副词, 表示向外, 外面; outward 还可用作形容词, an outward voyage.

pleasant, pleased, pleasing
pleasant 常用作定语, pleased, pleasing 常用作表语, pleased 主语常为人, pleasing 主语常为物, a pleasant trip, The trip is pleasing.

understanding, understandable
understanding 明白事理的, 能体谅的; understandable 可理解的, 能懂的, an understanding girl, an understandable mistake.
