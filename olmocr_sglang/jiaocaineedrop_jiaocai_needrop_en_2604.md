7. [解] 设 \( A(x, y) \), 则由题意可得 \( \frac{y}{x} = \frac{3}{2} \)。①

当 \( AO = AM \) 时，则 \( AO^2 = AM^2 \), 即 \( x^2 + y^2 = (13 - x)^2 + y^2 \)。②

由①②得
\[
\begin{align*}
x &= \frac{3}{2}x, \\
\frac{3}{2}x^2 + y^2 &= (13 - x)^2 + y^2.
\end{align*}
\]
解得
\[
\begin{align*}
x &= \frac{3}{2}, \\
y &= \frac{13}{4}.
\end{align*}
\]
或
\[
\begin{align*}
x &= -\frac{3}{2}, \\
y &= -\frac{13}{4}.
\end{align*}
\]
