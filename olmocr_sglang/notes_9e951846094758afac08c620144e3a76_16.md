7. 如图，梯形ABCD中，AB//CD，若AB=1，CD=2，AC=6，S_{△AOB}=1。求AD=______

解：∵AB//CD
∴△AOB≌△COD
即 \frac{AO}{CO} = \frac{AB}{CD} ∴AB=1，CD=2，AC=6
∴ \frac{AO}{CO} = \frac{1}{2} ⇒ CO = 2AO
∴AC = AO + CO = 3AO ⇒ 3AO = 6，AO = 2
∴ S_{△AOB} = (AB / CD)^2 = (1 / 2)^2 = 1 / 4 = S_{△COD}
∴ S_{△COD} = 4
∴ S_{△AOB} 与 S_{△BOC} (不同底，同高)
面积之比为 OA : OC = 1 : 2
∴ S_{△AOB} : S_{△BOC} = 1 : 2 ∴ S_{△AOB} = 1
∴ S_{△BOC} = 2 同理：S_{△AOD} 与 S_{△COD} (不同底，同高)
面积之比为 OA : OC = 1 : 2 ∴ S_{△AOD} : S_{△COD} = 1 : 2
