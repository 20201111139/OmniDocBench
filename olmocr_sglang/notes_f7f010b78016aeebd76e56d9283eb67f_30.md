look指从外表上看，seem指内心的判断；appear指给人以表面的印象。
appear wise, look like his father.
107. gather, collect
gather把分散的东西集中在一起，collect指精心地、有选择地进行收集。
collect stamps.
108. mean to do, mean doing
mean to do打算，想要做某事；mean doing意味着，意味着。
By this I mean giving the students more practice.
109. die from, die of
die from表示死于（枪）伤，虚弱，过度劳累，饮食过度等，die of表示死于疾病、饥饿、寒冷、年老、忧虑、失恋等精神因素。die of hunger and cold.
110. pay for, pay back, pay off
pay for为…付钱；pay back还钱，但不一定还清；pay off还清。
pay for the book, pay off the debt.
111. divide, separate
divide把一个整体分成几部分，separate把连在一起的个体分开。
divide the apple, separate the houses.
112. arrive, get, reach
arrive不及物动词，后接in（大地点），at（小地点）；get不及物动词后接to；reach及物动词，arrive in Beijing，get to Beijing，reach Beijing.
113. grow, plant
grow使某种植物在某地生长着或使其发展下去；plant移植，移栽已经长成秧苗的植物。Plant the trees, trees are growing.
114. manage, try
manage to do设法做成某事；try to do尽力去做某事，但不一定成功。
He tried to pass the exam, but he failed.
