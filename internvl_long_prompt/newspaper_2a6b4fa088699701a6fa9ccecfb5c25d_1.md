## ACTION: Notice of 12-month petition findings.
SUMMARY: We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, announce 12-month findings on petitions to list 10 species as endangered or threatened under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). After a review of the best available scientific and commercial information, we find that listing the Huachuca-Canelo Arizona treefrog, the Arkansas darter, black mudalia, Highlands tiger beetle, Dicamptus occidentalis (a species of ant), First Brothers' panic grass, Kentucky cave shrimp, Louisville cave beetle and Tatum Cave beetle, redhorse sucker, and Stephan's riffle beetle is not warranted at this time.
