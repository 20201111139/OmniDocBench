# Family Preservation and Assessment System
## Hillsborough County, Florida

### Summary
Hillsborough County's Family Preservation and Assessment System is a county-wide data-sharing platform in the children services sector. The purpose of the Family Preservation and Assessment System is to enable the prevention of abuse or neglect of a child, or a child in care. Hillsborough County, in collaboration with EY, has designed this tool to help realize the vision of preservation of families through targeted early intervention and support. The tool has been deployed in the community since March 2021.
