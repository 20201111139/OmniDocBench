## Local First
is a non-profit organization that leads the development of an economy grounded in local ownership that meets the basic needs of people, builds local wealth and social capital, functions in harmony with our ecosystem, and encourages a joyful community.

### Local First
support locally owned business

Local isn't a place on a map. It's people. It's reintegrating, our lives, connecting practical ways with the people we live and work with. Local First is another way of saying "People First."

Local isn't just a place on a map. It's people, their neighbors and their families, their businesses, farms, nonprofits, events, and recreational venues. Local is a community and all of the opportunities we create together. Challenges we face together. Local First is a passion for living and working together for sustainable community.

### When we say local, we mean:
- Locally Owned
- Headquartered in West Michigan
- Privately Held (not traded on the stock market)

### By eating locally, you will:
- Support our farmers
- Strengthen our local economy
- Ensure that you receive high quality food
- Cultivate community
- Eat sustainably while increasing healthy options.

## Why Local First?
1. Local First Engages. Local First engages and deepens relationships within community, which become more vibrant and resilient as a result.
2. Local First Preserves. Local First preserves physical and economic environments.
3. Local First Empowers. Local First empowers citizens to be genuine stakeholders, protecting local economic and environmental strengths.
4. Local First Strengthens. Local First strengthens cities and local business networks, when citizens and local businesses work together, business synergy and positive economic outcomes are more likely to occur.
5. Local First Preserves. Local First preserves the unique character of our communities and the businesses that have built successful economies.
6. Local First Energizes. Local First energizes and facilitates connections between customers and businesses, encouraging innovation, interaction, and discovery, making a more vibrant community.
7. Local First Empowers. Local First empowers citizens to be genuine stakeholders in the entire community.
