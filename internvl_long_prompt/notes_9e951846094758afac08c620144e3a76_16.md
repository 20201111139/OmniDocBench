7. 如图, 梯形ABCD中: AB//CD, 若AB=1, CD=2, AC=6, S△AOB=1, R//AD=___

解: ∵ AB//CD
∴ △AOB∽△COD
即 \(\frac{AO}{CO} = \frac{AB}{CD} \because AB=1, CD=2, AC=6\)
\(\therefore \frac{AO}{CO} = \frac{1}{2} \Rightarrow CO = 2AO\)
\(\therefore AC = AO + CO = 3AO \Rightarrow 3AO = 6 \Rightarrow AO = 2\)
\(\therefore S_{\Delta AOB} = \frac{1}{2} \times AB \times AO = \frac{1}{2} \times 1 \times 2 = 1\)
\(\therefore S_{\Delta COD} = 4\)
\(\because S_{\Delta AOB} \text{与} S_{\Delta BOC} \text{同底,同高}\)
\(\therefore S_{\Delta AOB} : S_{\Delta BOC} = OA : OC = 1 : 2\)
\(\therefore S_{\Delta BOC} = 2 \therefore S_{\Delta AOD} \text{与} S_{\Delta COD} \text{同底,同高}\)
\(\therefore S_{\Delta BOC} : S_{\Delta COD} = OA : OC = 1 : 2\)
