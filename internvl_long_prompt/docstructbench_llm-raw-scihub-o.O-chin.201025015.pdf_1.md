Mixed Cations and Structural Complexity in (Eu1-xCax)InGe3 and (Eu1-xCax)In2Ge3 — The First Two Members of the Homologous Series A2nIn2nGe3n (n = 1, 2,...; A: Ca, Sr, Ba, Eu, or Yb). — Eu2Ca7InGe4 (I), Eu2Ca4In3Ge11 (II), Eu3Ca7In2Ge3 (III), and Eu6Ca3In4Ge3 (IV) are synthesized from the elements in a molten metal flux (960 °C, 20 h). The compounds are characterized by single crystal XRD, magnetic susceptibility measurements, and TB-LMTO band structure calculations, (I) and (II) crys- tallize in the space group C2/m with Z = 2, and (III) and (IV) in the space group Pnma with Z = 4. The polyatomic substructures of the compounds are based on InGe3 edge-sharing tetrahedra, Ge2 dimers, and bridging In atoms in a nearly square planar environment. — (YOU, T.-S.; TOBASHI, P. H.; BOBEV*, S.; Inorg. Chem. 49 (2010) 4, 1773 - 1783; Dep. Chem. Biochem., Univ. Del., Newark, DE 19716, USA; Eng.) — W. Preweston
