# Federal Register / Vol. 89, No. 134 / Friday, July 12, 2024 / Notices

## The Ely District Record of Decision and Approved Resource Management Plan (ROD), dated August 20, 2008, as referenced in the August 6, 2008, Federal Register (73 FR 46843), page LR-8, page 67, objectives LR-8, page 66, LR-11, page 108-424, the Lincoln County Development, Recreation, and Conservation Act of 2004, and Public Law 109-432, the Tax Relief and Health Care Act of 2006, Title III—Recreation and Public Lands, and their disposal and management requirements, are not required for any other Federal purposes, and meet the intent of the WPCRDRA (Pub. L. 109-432), section 311(b)(1), provides that Federal land (as defined in section 102(1) of the Act) is withdrawn from all forms of entry, location, and withdrawal under the public land laws and mining laws; all minerals will be retained by the Federal Government. A Finding of No Significant Impact (FONSI) on June 7, 2013, a Decision Record, dated September 12, 2019, were completed in connection with this action. According to the LCRDRA (Pub. L. 108-424, section 102(1) identified within the Ely Resource Management Plan are withdrawn from location and entry under the mining laws and from operation under the mineral leasing and geothermal leasing laws until such time as the Secretary of the Interior (Secretary) terminates the withdrawal. In accordance with the LCRDRA and WPCRDRA, 85 percent of the LCRDRA and 85 percent of the WPCRDRA generated by this sale will be used for archaeological resources, recreation and wilderness planning, and other opportunities in Lincoln and White Pine County respectively. Additionally, five percent of the Nevada revenue will go to the General Fund and 10 percent of the revenue will go to Lincoln County respectively.

The following National Environmental Policy Act documents apply to this proposed land sale. Separate environmental assessments were prepared for the parcels in each county. The Lincoln County parcels are analyzed in DOI-BLM-NV-L030-2015-0027-EA (https://eplanning.blm.gov/eplanning-ui/project/56256/6027) and 0027-EA (https://eplanning.blm.gov/eplanning-ui/project/724965710).
