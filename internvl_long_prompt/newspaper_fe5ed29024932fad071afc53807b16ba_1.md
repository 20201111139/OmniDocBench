#### Raisins Produced From Grapes Grown in California; Final Free and Reserve Percentages for 2003-04 Crop Natural (Sun-Dried) Seedless Raisins

**ACTION:** Final rule.

**SUMMARY:** The Department of Agriculture (USDA) is adopting, as a final rule, without change, an interim final rule that established final volume percentages for Natural (sun-dried) Seedless (NS) raisins covered under the marketing order for California raisins (order). The producer price differentials from the handling of 2003-04 crop NS raisins grown in California and is locally administered by the Raisin Administrative Committee (Committee). The interim final rule was published on April 27, 2004. The 70 percent free and 30 percent reserve percentages are intended to help stabilize raisin supplies and prices, and strengthen marketing conditions. This rule continues in effect the volume percentages previously established for the 2003-04 crop.

**EFFECTIVE DATE:** Effective September 15, 2004.
