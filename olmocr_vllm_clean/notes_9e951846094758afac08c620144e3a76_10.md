【解】：DE//BC
∴∠1=∠2 又 ∠A=∠C
∴△ADE ~ △ABC
∴$\frac{AD}{AB} = \frac{2}{3}$ 为相似比
∴$\frac{S_{\triangle ADE}}{S_{\triangle ABC}} = \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^2 = \frac{4}{9}$
设 $S_{\triangle ADE} = 4x$，则 $S_{\triangle ABC} = 9x$
∴$S_{\text{四边形DBCE}} = 9x - 4x = 5x$
∴$\frac{S_{\triangle ADE}}{S_{\text{四边形DBCE}}} = \frac{4x}{5x} = \frac{4}{5}$
