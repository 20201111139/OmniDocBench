

看到“平行”，再看到“周长”

相似比=1：周长比

\[
\frac{AD}{BC} : \triangle MAD \sim \triangle MBC
\]

相似比为 \(\frac{AD}{BC} = \frac{8}{16} = \frac{4}{5} = \frac{MA}{MB} = \frac{MD}{MC}\)

\[
\frac{\triangle MAD}{\triangle MBC} = \frac{4}{5} \quad \text{(1)}
\]

\[
\frac{MA}{MB} = \frac{4}{5} \quad \text{和} \quad \frac{MA}{MA+4} = \frac{4}{5} \quad \Rightarrow \quad MA = 16
\]

\[
\frac{MD}{MC} = \frac{4}{5} \quad \text{和} \quad \frac{MD}{MD+5} = \frac{4}{5} \quad \Rightarrow \quad MD = 20
\]

\[
\triangle MAD 周长 = MA + AD + MD = 16 + 8 + 20 = 44 \quad \text{(2)}
\]

由（1）和（2）知，\(\triangle MBC\)周长为55
