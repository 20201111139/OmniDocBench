### 3. 序数词，表示顺序的数词。

| 1st  | 2nd  | 3rd  | 4th  | 5th  | 6th  | 7th  | 8th  |
|------|------|------|------|------|------|------|------|
| first | second | third | fourth | fifth | sixth | seventh | eighth |
| 9th  | 10th | 11th | 12th | 13th | 14th | 15th | 16th |
| 17th | 18th | 19th | twentieth | 21st | twenty-second |
| 100th | one hundred and one |

### 4. 数词的用法:

1) 表示年份: 1902: twenty thousand and two; 1976: nineteen seventy-six.
2) 表示日期: 1902年11月2日: Nov. 11th, 1902.
3) 表示时刻: 5:15: five fifteen or a quarter past five; 8:30: eight thirty or half past eight; 10:45: ten forty-five or a quarter to eleven.
4) 表示编号: Room 105, Room One, O, Five; Bus No. 13, Bus Number Thirteen; P. 5: Page Five; Tel. No. 765-2569, Telephone Number Seven-six-five-eight-six-five-nine.
5) 小数的读法: 5.7: five point seven; 0.6: zero point six.
6) “半”的表达: 1/2: half; 1/2 hour: half an hour; 1.5 hours: one and a half hours or one hour and a half.
7) 序数词前面加 the 时，表示顺序，加 a/an 时表示“再一····”如: The third lesson is rather difficult. (第三课相当难) / Should we read the text a third time? (我们把课文读第三遍，好吗？)
