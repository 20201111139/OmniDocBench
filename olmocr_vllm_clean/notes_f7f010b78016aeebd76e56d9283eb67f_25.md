55. the people, a people

56. the people 指人，a people 指民族。The Chinese is a peace-loving people.

57. it, one

58. this

59. that, this

60. that 作上文所提到的，this 作下文所要说的。I was in. That’s why...

61. none, nothing, no one

62. How many…? How much…? none

63. anyone, any one

64. anyone 指人，不能接 of；any one 指人，物均可，可接 of，any one of you.

65. who, what

66. who 指姓名或关系，what 指职业或地位。Who is your dad? He is a teacher.

67. what, which

68. what 的选择基础是无限制的，which 有一定的范围内进行选择。What do you prefer, bananas or apples?

69. other, another

70. other 后接名词复数，another 后接名词单数。Other students, another student.

71. not a little, not a bit

72. not a little 非常，not a bit 一点也不。I’m not a bit tired. 我一点也不累。

73. many, much, a lot of

74. many 和可数名词连用，much 和不可数名词连用，a lot of 可数。

75. I haven’t many books.
