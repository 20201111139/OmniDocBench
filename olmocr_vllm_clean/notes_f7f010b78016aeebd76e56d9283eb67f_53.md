(1) My pen is much more expensive than yours. (我的钢笔比你的贵多了)
(2) 周名、月名和节名前一般不用
(3) He was born on Monday, February 18, 1995. (他出生于1995年2月18日，星期一)
(4) 量词前不用量词不表示数量的类别词不用。
如：Men are cleverer than monkeys. (人比猴子聪明)
(5) 三餐前不用
如：We have breakfast at home and lunch at school.
(我们在家吃早饭，在校吃午饭)
(6) 假日名词前一般不用
如：On Children's Day, the boys often get presents from their parents.
(在儿童节，这天男孩常收到父母的礼物)
(7) 某些名词前不用
如：The children play football on Saturday afternoons.
(孩子们星期六下午踢足球)
(8) 城市的名词，主要建筑物名称前不用
如：They are now at People's Cinema. (他们此刻在人民电影院)
(9) 一些习语用语中不用
如：① at/to/from/out of/after/for school; ② on top of;
③ in/so/to/after class; ④ in/so/out of/into bed;
⑤ after/last/out of/so work; ⑥ go to school/bed;
⑦ on show/display/duty/watch; ⑧ in front of;
⑨ at/sea; ⑩ in/out of/hospital;
⑪ in/from/down/to town; ⑫ at all;
⑬ at/from home; ⑭ on/in time; ⑮ at first/last/once;
⑯ eat for/to/breakfast/lunch/supper; ⑰ take care of;
