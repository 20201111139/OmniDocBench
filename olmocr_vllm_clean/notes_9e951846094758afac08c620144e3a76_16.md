7. 如图，梯形ABCD中，AB∥CD. 若AB=1，CD=2，AC=6，S_{△AOB}=1，则AO=____

解：∵AB∥CD
∴△A0B∽△COD
即 \frac{AO}{CD} = \frac{AB}{CD} \because AB=1，CD=2，AC=6
∴ AO = \frac{1}{2} \Rightarrow CD = 2AO

又：AC = AO + CO = 3AO ⇒ 3AO = 6；AO = 2
∴ \frac{S_{△A0B}}{S_{△COD}} = (\frac{AB}{CD})^2 = (\frac{1}{2})^2 = \frac{1}{4} = \frac{1}{S_{△COD}}
∴ S_{△COD} = 4
\because S_{△A0B} 与 S_{△B0C} (不同底, 同高)
且底之比为 OA:OC = 1:2 \implies S_{△A0B} = 1
\therefore S_{△B0C} = 2
同理：S_{△A0D} 与 S_{△COD} (不同底, 同高)
且底之比为 OA:OC = 1:2 \implies S_{△A0D} = S_{△COD} = 1:2
