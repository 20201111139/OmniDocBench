Is a non-profit organization that leads the development of an economy grounded in local ownership that meets the basic needs of people, builds local wealth and social capital, functions in harmony with our ecosystem, and encourages joyful community.

## Why Local First?

1. Local First Earnings. It creates and despires relationships within a community, which becomes more climate through collaboration and mutual investment.
2. Local First Humanities. Physical and economic development occur on a scale that matters people and encourages beauty in natural and manmade environments.
3. Local First Properties. When more citizens are greater relationships, protecting local economy, and environmental value is of mutual interest.
4. Local First Emergencies. When citizens and local businesses work together, it stabilizes positive development and the community has structural integrity to withstand economic detentories.
5. Local First Iavents. Sustainable local economies and ecoprimes give into markets that are greater than the sum of their parts.
6. Local First Encourages. Intimate connections between consumers and businesses facilitate innovation, interaction, and discovery, making for a more climate and resilient local economy.
7. Local First Enriches. Entervating success in customers, employees, and the local environment makes the entire community wealthier.
