Mixed Cations and Structural Complexity in $\left(\mathbf{E u}_{1-4} \mathbf{C a}_{4}\right)_{4} \mathbf{I n}_{3} \mathbf{G e}_{4}$ and $\left(\mathbf{E u}_{1-4} \mathbf{C a}_{4}\right)_{3} \mathbf{I n}_{2} \mathbf{G e}_{3}$ - The First Two Members of the Homologous Series $\mathbf{A}_{2(n+m)} \mathbf{n}_{2(n+m} \mathbf{G e}_{2(n+m)}\left(\mathbf{n}, \mathbf{m}=\mathbf{1}, \mathbf{2}, \cdots \infty ; \mathbf{A} ; \mathbf{C a}, \mathbf{S r}, \mathbf{B a}, \mathbf{E u}, \mathbf{o r} \mathbf{Y b}\right),-$ $\mathrm{Eu}_{1-23} \mathrm{Ca}_{2-77} \mathrm{In}_{3} \mathrm{Ge}_{4}$ (I), $\mathrm{Eu}_{2-60} \mathrm{Ca}_{1-40} \mathrm{In}_{3} \mathrm{Ge}_{4}$ (II), $\mathrm{Eu}_{0-30} \mathrm{Ca}_{2-70} \mathrm{In}_{2} \mathrm{Ge}_{3}$ (III), and $\mathrm{Eu}_{0-60} \mathrm{Ca}_{2-34} \mathrm{In}_{2} \mathrm{Ge}_{3}$ (IV) are synthesized from the elements in a molten In metal flux $\left(960^{\circ} \mathrm{C}, 20 \mathrm{~h}\right)$. The compounds are characterized by single crystal XRD, magnetic susceptibility measurements, and TB-LMTO band structure calculations. (I) and (II) crystallize in the space group $\mathrm{C} 2 / \mathrm{m}$ with $\mathrm{Z}=2$, and (III) and (IV) in the space group Pnma with $\mathrm{Z}=4$. The polyanionic substructures of the compounds are based on $\mathrm{InGe}_{4}$ edge--sharing tetrahedra, $\mathrm{Ge}_{2}$ dimers, and bridging In atoms in a nearly square planar environment. - (YOU, T.-S.; TOBASH, P.
