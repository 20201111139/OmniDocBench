## Mixed Cations and Structural Complexity in (Eu1xCax)4In3Ge4 and (Eu1xCax)3In2Ge3 - The First Two Members of the Homologous Series A2[n+m] In2n+m Ce2[n+m] (n, m = 1, 2, "00; A: Ca, Sr, Ba, Eu, or Yb). -Eu1,23Ca277In3Ge4 (I), Eu2.60Ca1,40In3Ge4 (II), Eu0.30Ca2.70In2Ge3 (II), and Eu066Ca234In2Ge3 (IV) are synthesized from the elements in a molten In metal flux (960 ℃, 20 h). The compounds are characterized by single crystal XRD, magnetic susceptibility measurements, and TB-LMTO band structure calculations. (I) and (II) crystallize in the space group C2/m with Z = 2, and (III) and (IV) in the space group Pnma with Z = 4. The polyanionic substructures of the compounds are based on InGe, edge--sharing tetrahedra, Ge2 dimers, and bridging In atoms in a nearly square planar environment. - (YOU, T.- S .; TOBASH, P.
