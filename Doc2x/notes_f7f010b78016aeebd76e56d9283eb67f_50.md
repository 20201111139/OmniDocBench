## 4. 数词的用法:

1) 表示年份:2002 :twenty thousand and two ; 976 :ninetoen seventy six. 2) 表示日期:12月1日:Dec. 1st 或 the first of December； 2002年11月8日:Nov. 8 th. 2002. 3)表示时刻:5:15→five fifteen 或 a quarter past five； 8:30→eight thirty或 half Past eight ; 10:45→ ten forty-five B a quarter to eleven. 4)表示偏号:Room 105 ~ Room one 0 five ; Bus No.13 → Bus Number Thirtoon; P. 5 -> Page Five ; Tel. No. 7658659 -> Telephone Number seven - six - five- eight - six- five- nine.

5) 小数的读法:5.7 → five point seven j 0.16 → zero point one six .

6) "半"的表达:1/2→half；半小时→half an hour；1.5小时→one and a half hours 或 one hour and a hair.

7) 序数词前面加the时,表示顺序,如 a/an 时表示“再一、又一”。如:The third lesson is rather difficult.(第三课相当难)/ shall we read the text a third time? (我们把保文读写三遍,好吗?)
