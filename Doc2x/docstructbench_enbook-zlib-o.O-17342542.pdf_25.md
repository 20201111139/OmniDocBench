Enclosed Basic Switches North American limit switches are also available in an enclosed basic version. These switches are designated by the catalog number 3SE03-EB. Enclosed basic switches are preconfigured with a plunger actuator, booted plunger, roller lever, booted roller lever, roller plunger, or a booted roller plunger.
