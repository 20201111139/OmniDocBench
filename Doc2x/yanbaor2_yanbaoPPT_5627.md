## 5 trends reshaping China's consumer market

1 The middle class continues to rise

Double-digit growth of upper-middle and high-income households is powering robust consumption growth

3 Smarter choices, but not trading down

Consumers are switching to cheaper channels and more aggressively seeking discounts and promotions

5 Local companies

are winning

Consumers are choosing local brands for their quality and innovation and not just for cheaper prices, or out of a sense of national pride

2 Premiumization maintains momentum

Affluent consumers prefer premium over mass-market brands

4 It's all about the product

Consumers are savvy and know how to educate themselves about the technical specifications of their favorite products on social media
