(2004) documented the same pattern of chitinase production with the use of different carbon and nitrogen source for the fungus Trichoderma harzianum. Maximum chitinase activity was observed in Medium III constituting colloidal chitin as carbon source and Yeast extract as a nitrogen source, compared to the other three media. The exact mechanism of induction of this enzyme by extra nitrogen source is not known but it is assumed that either Yeast extract supports the mycelial growth and hence enhancement in the initial growth leads to more chitinase production or it could be due to the presence of oligomers of GlcNAc which can directly induce the chitinase gene (De Moraes et al., 2003).
