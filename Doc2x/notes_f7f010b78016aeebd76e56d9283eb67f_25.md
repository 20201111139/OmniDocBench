156: the prople . A people

the people 指人, a people 指民族. The Chinese is a peace-loving people.

57. it , one

it同一物体, one 同类不同一. I lost my pen. I have to buy a new one.

58. that. this

that 指代上文所提到的, this导出下文所要说的.

I was in . That's why...

59. none , nothing , no one

none 强调有多少, nothing , no one 强调有没有, nothing 指物, 加以指人

- How many... / How much...? - hone

bo. anyone , any one

anyone 指人,不能接口。当, any one 指人,物均可,可接 of anyone of you.

bl. who, what

who 指姓名或关系, what指职业或地位

What is your dad? He is a teacher.

62. what which

What 的选择基础是无限制的。which 在一定范围内进行选择.

which do you prefer, bananas or apples?

63. Other . Another

Other 后接名词复数, a mother 后接名词单数. Other students. another student.

64: not a little , not a bit.

not a little 非常, not a bit 一点也不.

I'm not a bit tired. 我一点儿也不累

65: many, much, a lot of

many 和可数名词连用. much 和不可数名词连用, a lot of 可数.

但不用于否定句: I haven't many books.
