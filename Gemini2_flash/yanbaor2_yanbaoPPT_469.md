Many grocers have made great progress
on analytical maturity. Leaders in
analytics have tackled the majority of
fundamental use cases, such as pricing,
mass promotion, and assortment
optimization. Now they have increasingly
turned their focus to pursuing new
use cases along the value chain and
improving existing use cases-for
example, by using more granular, real-
time data. These efforts are often driven
by a strong analytics unit, but the level
of adoption across a business varies.
The best analytics solution does not
help if it is not used and understood by
the respective decision makers, such as
category managers.
In many cases, organizational maturity is

adoption and realizing the full potential
of analytics. It encompasses both the
