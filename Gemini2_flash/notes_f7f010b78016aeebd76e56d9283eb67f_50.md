4.数词的用法:
1)表示年份: 2002: twenty thousand and two; 1976: nineteen seventy-six.
2)表示日期:12月1日: Dec. 1st 或 the first of December;
2002年11月8日:Nov-8th, 2002.
3)表示时刻: 5:15→ five fifteen 或 a quarter past five;
8:30→ eight thirty 或 half past eight i
10:45→ ten forty-five 或 a quarter to eleven.
4)表示编号:Room 105→ Room one O five; Bus No.13→ Bus Number Thirteen;
P.5→ Page Five; Tel. No. 7658659→ Telephone Number seven-six-
five-eight-six-five-nine.
5)小数的读法:5.7→ five point seven; 0.16→ zero point one six.
6)“半”的表达:1/2→ half; 半小时→ half an hour; 1.5小时→ one and a half
hours 或 one hour and a halt.
7)序数词前面加the时,表示顺序,加a/an时表示“再一、又一”。如:The third
lesson is rather difficult. (第三课相当难)/ Shall we read the text a third
time?(我们把课文读第三遍,好吗?)
